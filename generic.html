HomeFinder: Find the Best City for You

Using Python

This project was made with my friends as part of the Texas A&M University 2020 Datathon. Our final product was a heroku web application to gauge the best city/home based on user-specified metrics and sklearn's davies_bouldin_score attribute along with KMeans clustering. After gauging the otpimal "home(s)" for a user, we visualized these locations and used web-scraping to display images of a user's ideal city. We won 3rd place in our category. An Investigation of the California Wildfire Crisis

Using Python and R

I wrote a comprehensive article for DataRes at UCLA that looks at California wildfire growth over the last few years, and the role climate change has played in exacerbating the current wildfire crisis. What amazed me while writing this article was how a select few number of counties in California account for so much of the annual wildfire destruction.
